Our Story

The Gowda Legacy

Founded in 1995 in the heart of Bangalore, Gowda's Tea Co. began as a humble tea stall with a simple mission: to serve the perfect cup of chai. What started as a family's passion project has blossomed into a beloved institution, cherished by tea enthusiasts across the city.
