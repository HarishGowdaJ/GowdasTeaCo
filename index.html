Our Heritage

Founded in 1995, Gowda's Tea Co. started as a small tea stall in Bangalore. What began as a passion for perfecting the art of tea-making has now grown into a beloved destination for tea enthusiasts across the city.

We source our tea leaves directly from the finest estates and use traditional recipes that have been passed down through generations of the Gowda family.
