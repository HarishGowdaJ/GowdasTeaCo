Our Story

Welcome to Gowda's Tea Co.

Founded in 2010, Gowda's Tea Co. began as a small family-owned tea shop in the heart of Bangalore. What started as a humble endeavor to share our love for authentic Indian tea has blossomed into a beloved destination for tea enthusiasts from around the world.

Our journey has been guided by a simple philosophy: to serve only the finest, ethically-sourced teas while creating a warm, welcoming space where community and conversation can flourish.

Today, we continue to honor our roots while constantly innovating to bring you new and exciting tea experiences. Every cup we serve is a testament to our commitment to quality, tradition, and the art of tea-making.
